## Positioning

**Who Hannah is:** Startup operator and B2B marketer building in public
**What she does:** Helps ambitious builders in their 20s and 30s construct nonlinear careers, businesses, and wealth across multiple lanes
**Voice:** Sharp, direct, no bullshit. Builder energy, not escape energy. In the trenches, not above it.

## Target Audience: Alex

High-achieving person in their 20s-30s with elite credentials (or equivalent scrappiness), working in finance/consulting/tech/startups, who has a 9-5 AND is actively building something alongside it.

**Primary focus:** Has both a day job and a side project, figuring out how to make them compound instead of compete.

**What Alex needs:**
- Narrative clarity (how to talk about yourself when you don't fit in a box)
- Positioning that compounds (personal brand that opens doors, not just gets likes)
- Builder community (people constructing something alongside their 9-5)
- Real tradeoffs, real numbers (not just inspiration)
- The flywheel framework (career + content + income streams reinforcing each other)

## Content Pillars

1. **Creator Economy Strategist** - Strategic takes on creator businesses, business model breakdowns. Hannah's edge: B2B marketer lens + MBA + startup ops.
2. **Personal Branding for Reluctant High-Achievers** - Permission-giving, tactical how-to for credentialed people who feel weird about self-promotion. Hannah's edge: she WAS that person.
3. **5-9 Entrepreneur BTS** - Community moments, real week-in-the-life, personal reflections. Hannah's edge: she's living it, not coaching from the other side.

## Posting Cadence

3x per week:
- **Tuesday:** Builder insight - flywheel in action, real tradeoffs, what she's learning
- **Thursday:** Tactical/shareable - frameworks, contrarian takes, "here's what works"
- **Weekend:** Personal story - career pivots, messy middle, lessons, reflections

## Content Principles

**Show:** The flywheel in practice, what separates builders who execute from planners, real tradeoffs, why narrative and positioning matter in the AI era, B2B storytelling as a superpower.

**Stop:** Anchoring on golden handcuffs/escape as primary frame, generic career advice, copying what 100K+ creators do.

**Remember:** Less but better > more and forgettable. Lived experience > theoretical frameworks. Specific > generic.

## Key Metrics

- Saves and dwell time > likes
- Comments with 2+ words > vanity engagement
- Profile visits > raw impressions
- Follower quality > follower count
